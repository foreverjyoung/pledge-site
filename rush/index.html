Rush Schedule

To rush Alpha Phi Omega, you must fulfill the following 3 requirements:

  • Attend 1 Info Night
  • Attend 1 Additional Rush Event
  • Attend Ritual
  • Strongly recommended but not mandatory: Meet the Chapter and Interviews

As a general note, the more active brothers you meet, the more likely you will be invited to our fraternity, so we encourage you to attend as many events as you can!

We look forward to meeting you! Please contact Nicki Bartak at rush@calaphio.com for any questions or concerns.
