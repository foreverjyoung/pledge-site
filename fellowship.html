Fellowship and Brotherhood

"Never look down on someone unless you're helping them up."

- Unknown

The brotherhood and camaraderie that exists among our members sets us apart from any other service organization. We believe helping others can be even more fun alongside familiar faces and friends as we learn and grow from our experiences together. Fellowships are social events and bondings to foster friendship and community among fraternity brothers and encompass a diverse range of recreational activities, including playing sports, eating, attending Berkeley events, exploring the Bay area, and other spectacular events such as skydiving. At the end of the day, we are not just individuals who do service together, but friends who can trust and depend on each other. These friendships come to form the glue that sustains our membership for years to come.
