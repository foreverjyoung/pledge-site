Alpha Phi Omega

Be a leader. Be a friend. Be of service.

Leadership

Alpha Phi Omega develops the leadership skills of its members through shared leadership. Our members have the opportunity to serve as a chapter officer or to plan and execute any number of volunteer projects and social programs, as well as the chance to participate in various leadership development programs.

Friendship

Brotherhood is the spirit of friendship. It implies respect, honesty and dependability. It means that we overlook differences as we join together in unselfish service. It means listening to Brothers whose views on issues might differ from our own. It means working closely with people whom under other circumstances we might not choose as our friends.

Service

Participation in our service program helps make Alpha Phi Omega such a unique fraternity. We work with many local non-profit organizations such as Project Open Hand and participate in many large-scale service events such as The Color Run, Nike's Women Marathon, etc.
